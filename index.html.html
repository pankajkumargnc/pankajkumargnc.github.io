I'm a non-teaching staff member at Guru Nanak College, Dhanbad — part of the Binod Bihari Mahto Koylanchal University system. Outside my official duties, I'm a self-taught developer who builds real, production-grade software to solve real problems.

My biggest project — Jharkhand Pay Master Pro v8.0 — is a comprehensive salary management system built from scratch using React 19, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS. What started as a simple calculator for personal use grew into a full-featured platform with 14 modules, multi-profile management, official document generation, and premium Excel exports.

I have deep domain expertise in government pay rules — 7th CPC, 8th CPC projections, MACP career progression, NPS contributions, GSLI, income tax comparison (Old vs New regime), retirement & gratuity calculations, and DA history tracking since 2016.

This combination of technical skill and domain knowledge is rare — and it's what makes the software genuinely useful, not just technically functional.

// Built for Jharkhand Government University & College Non-Teaching Staff

A production-grade salary management system developed entirely from scratch. Covers every dimension of government salary — from 7th Pay Commission fixation to 8th Pay projection, MACP career progression, NPS retirement corpus, income tax comparison, official salary bills, and premium styled Excel exports.

Multi-profile system — unlimited employees, grid/list view, import/export Excel
Dashboard with SVG donut charts, DA sparklines, and live payroll statistics
7th Pay Fixation with 6th CPC conversion & interactive pay matrix
MACP Calculator — auto level upgrades at 10, 20, 30 service years
Retirement planner — DOB-based auto-calculation, full year-wise trail
Official salary bill PDF · Annual statement · Form 16 estimate
Premium Excel exports — themed headers, alternating rows, auto column widths
Fully responsive — mobile bottom nav, tablet & desktop optimized
Financial Planner — NPS corpus (4 return rates), 10-yr timeline, health score
